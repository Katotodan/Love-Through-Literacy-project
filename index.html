Welcome to the Love Though Literacy Project

Who We Are

The Love Through Literacy Project seeks to nurture joyful and fluent readerss in Uganda. Acquiring a variety of engaging books for children is a challenge for many famillies. The Love Though Literacy Profect provides children and adults with access to great reading books in a setting where they can interact with caring and qualified adults who help with the development of the reading skills and strategies.


Nurturing Lifelong Readers One World at a Time !

Our services

TUTORING IN READING & WRITING

We offer private lessons to children ages 6-18 and those lessons cover the following topics:
- Learnig Letter Sounds and Blending for Beginning Readers
- Practicing Sight words High Frequency Words
- Developing Reading Comprehension Skills
- Personal Narrative, and Persuasive Writing
- Supporting Development fo Research Skills

BOOK CLUBS

Weekly reading sessions offer children the opportunity to participate in story times and games related to literacy.

TRAINING FOR ADULTS

If you are interested in being able to support children in their learning journeys as readers this is a great opportunity for you. We coach teachers and parents in order to provide the foundational skill that are necessary to assist young readers.
